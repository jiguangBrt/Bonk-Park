## 🎨 Game Vision: The "Squash & Stretch" Survival
**Bonk Park** is a vibrant, cartoon-style 2D physics-brawler. You play as a bouncy character in a playground filled with "Bullies" (AI) trying to catch you. You have no way to fight back directly—instead, you use your **Magic Friction Shoes** to make the Bullies slip, slide, and go **BONK** against the walls!

The aesthetic is bright, bouncy, and chaotic, featuring "POW!" and "BAM!" visual effects whenever an enemy hits a wall.

## 🕹️ How to Play: The "Physics Prankster" Controls

### 1. The Slingshot (Right Hand - Mouse)
*   **Directional Thrust:** Your character doesn't just walk; they are pulled by an invisible rubber band! Move your **Mouse** to aim your acceleration.

### 2. The Magic Tiles (Left Hand - A / S / Space)
You can instantly change how the ground feels beneath your feet:
*   **[A] Super Sticky Mode (High Friction):** Your character gets "heavy" and gains 100% grip. Perfect for making sharp, sudden turns to dodge a charging Bully.
*   **[S] Slippery Slide Mode (Low Friction):** The floor turns into ice! Use this to build up massive speed and zoom across the park like a rocket.
*   **[Space] Normal Shoes:** Back to regular playground physics.

### 3. The Juice Meter (Energy Bar)
*   Changing the physics takes "Juice." Your energy bar (The Juice Meter) drains while you use Sticky or Slippery modes. 
*   It refills when you're just "chilling" in Normal Shoes, forcing you to time your pranks perfectly!

## 🤖 The Playground Bullies (AI Archetypes)
You are being chased by 3 to 5 high-difficulty AI "Bullies" with distinct personalities:

1.  **Dash the Dog (The Hound):** Pure energy, no brains. He charges straight at you at high speeds. He’s the easiest to trick into a **"Mega Bonk"** by dodging at the last second.
2.  **Pointy the Nerd (The Interceptor):** A super-smart AI that calculates where you *will* be. He tries to cut you off. You have to use **Sticky Mode** to suddenly change direction and break his brain!
3.  **Big Wall-y (The Herder):** A large, slow bully that tries to corner you against the park fences, making it easier for Dash and Pointy to catch you.

## ✨ The "Bonk" System
*   **The Big Bonk:** When an enemy is charging at you and you dodge using your friction fields, they can't stop in time and hit the wall. 
*   **Feedback:** The wall shakes, a loud **"BONK!"** sound plays, and stars spin around the enemy's head while they are stunned.
*   **Scoring:** 
    *   **Stayin' Alive:** Your score ticks up for every second you survive in the park.
    *   **Bonk Bonus:** Huge points for every Bully you trick into hitting a wall!
